

This is Version 4 of Zool Linux project , 
Zool 4 Supports better networking , newer software and utilities . 

This Commands are supported : 
sh             chgrp      e2fsck       ifconfig  mkdosfs       rm
umount         chmod      fdformat     init      mke2fs        rmdir
fsck.ext2      chown      find         insmod    mkreiserfs    rmmod
fsck.ext3      chroot     free         kill      mkswap        telnet
mkfs.ext2      clear      fsck         killall   mount         touch
mkfs.ext3      cp         halt         ln        mv            
ash            date       hdparm       ls        ping
cat            dd         head         lsmom     reboot
cfdisk         dmesg      hostname     mkdir     reiserfsck

"set_mod" : install your computer devices with their kernel modules . 

example : 

#set_net
